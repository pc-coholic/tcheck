Changelog:
Version 1.0
  • Initial Release
  • More 'proof of concept' than usefull extension
  • Checks at SIXT-Station "Munich Airport" for an rental car from Today+1 10 o'clock until Today+2 10 o'clock
  • Known bug (to be fixed in 1.01): You can't change the station nor date or other data
  • Known bug (to be fixed in 1.01): Links to the SIXT-website won't transport any useful information (like prefill the booking-mask)
  • Thanks to Christopher Finke for the tickerline code (Go checkout his RRS-Ticker at https://addons.mozilla.org/de/firefox/addon/2325/)
  • Thanks to Philipp Schmieder from http://www.vanbittern.de/ for the icon
